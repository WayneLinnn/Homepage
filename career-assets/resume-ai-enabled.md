# AI-enabled Software Resume Focus

## Target roles

- Software Engineer, AI Products
- AI Engineer
- Applied AI Engineer
- ML Engineer

## What to lead with

- Software engineering first, AI capability second
- Python, model workflows, evaluation, and deployment
- Ability to connect models to APIs, products, and users
- Delivery mindset: logging, permissions, and operational safety

## What to avoid

- Positioning yourself as a pure research scientist
- Listing AI tools without business or product context
- Overselling MLOps depth you cannot explain in interview

## Suggested summary line

Software engineer with Java backend experience and growing AI product capability across Python, RAG workflows, model evaluation, and deployment-oriented delivery.
