# AI Operations Framework for a Small Company

## Core rule

Humans own decisions and accountability. AI expands execution capacity.

## Human-owned areas

- Positioning
- Pricing
- Sales approval
- Financial decisions
- Legal and compliance decisions
- High-risk customer responses

## AI-supported areas

- Research
- Drafting outbound content
- Converting meeting notes into scope drafts
- First-pass code, tests, and docs
- FAQ support and ticket triage
- Weekly reporting and operating summaries

## Control points

- Define what can run automatically
- Define what needs human approval
- Set cost and usage limits
- Keep logs for customer-facing outputs
- Escalate ambiguous or high-risk cases to a human
